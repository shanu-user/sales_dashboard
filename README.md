Project Overview:

This project focuses on building an interactive and visually cohesive sales dashboard to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as sales trends, product performance, and customer segmentation.

The dashboard combines Seaborn (statistical visualization) and Plotly (interactive charts) to deliver both analytical depth and interactivity.

Objectives:
Analyze sales trends over time
Evaluate product-wise revenue contribution
Perform customer segmentation based on spending behavior
Visualize data distribution and variability
Build an interactive and visually appealing dashboard

Key Visualizations:
1. Sales Trend
Line chart showing monthly revenue progression
Helps identify growth patterns and fluctuations
2. Product Performance
Bar chart of revenue by product
Identifies top-performing and low-performing products
3. Customer Segmentation
Box plot categorizing customers into:
Low
Medium
High
Premium
Shows spending distribution across segments
4. Revenue Distribution
Violin plot for product-wise revenue spread
Highlights variability and skewness in sales
5. Interactive Charts (Plotly)
Interactive bar and line charts
Features:
Hover insights
Dynamic visualization

Key Insights:
Revenue is heavily concentrated in top products like laptops and phones
Premium customers contribute significantly higher revenue compared to other segments
Sales trend shows fluctuations with peak growth periods
Product revenue distribution indicates high variability across categories
